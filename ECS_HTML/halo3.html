What Makes Halo 3 Fun?

Halo 3 is a very flawed game, it has many bugs and glitches. The campain is fun, and has variying levels of duficulty. In my opinion it has some of the best graphics out of a Halo game. The scenery is on point for a game made in 2007. It also has one of gamings most iconic weapon, the Plasma Sword.

Combat and Weapons

The combat system could be improved, but you forget about that when going the the campain. Its super fun to cut through Grunts with a plasm sword, like a hot knife to butter. Its even fun to use the iconic assult rife Master Cheif use's through the franchise. This game adds a new feature to the serise, Dual Weilding. This is by far the best mechanic in the game.

Maps

Playing the maps is the equivilant of walking thorugh wonder land. You gain a sense of nastalgia, a sort of warm feeling. Its super cool to walk through the old maps with a friend. Everytime i go back i find something new that changes how you play on the that map. The map sizes range from super small, to extreamly large.

Multiplayer

I dont know how many time's ive gone back and played Multiplyer campain, or sniper dueled someone. Its one of those games that you can go back to every now and again with a group of friends and just slaughter each other in a versus match. It never gets old.
