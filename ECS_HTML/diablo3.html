What Makes Diablo 3 Fun?

This is a game of many cards, from trading with villagers, to roaming through deadly dungeons with flying kingly skeletions. But by far the funest part of the game is the interaction with the NPC's. You get to learn so much about each characters, and their individual lives. Sometimes you can go around and complete quests and they give you more information about themselves.

Fighting

The combat in this game is really fun. it works really well with the top down aspect of the game. Each act has different levels of diffuclty. Weapons, Armor, and Tools have varying levels of rarity, from Common to Legendary.

Multiplayer

Multiplyer is the great in this game. Its a little choppy when you player from different houses but with the amount of fun you have while doing it, it makes up for it. Each act is leveled to account how many players, the more players the higher the level. It makes it more challegening and fun.

Armor, Weapons, and Tools

Like i said earlier, the weapons system has rarities, from Common to Legendary. But on top of that they have class restrictions. Warriors can only use the long swords, and archers can only use the crossbows. This posses interseting challenges, because most classes that have power weapons have little health or mana. Its all about balance in this game, its fun to see when your team mate isn't balanced.
