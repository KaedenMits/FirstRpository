What Makes Minecraft Fun?

Minecraft is a game for all ages. No matter what you do, youll find it entertaining if you give it enough time. You can an endless amount of unique exploration. An infinate world with endless posibilities. The only thing that would make it better is more bosses.

Multiplayer

This is about the best part of the game 10 fold. Almost nothing compares to the multiplayer aspect of the game. Gaming with your friends is just better. You can play tons of mini games, and start a new survival world, and play modded minecraft with friends. Its a lot easier to race against someone else.

Modded Minecraft

When you can't play with friends its nice to have a thing to fall back on, Modded Minecraft. There are lots of differnt challenge modpacks to test your minecraft skills. You can literaly add anything you want to minecraft with a few clicks of a button. Its even better with friends.

Hardcore Mode

If survival isn't your speed and want more of a chellenge, we got you. Turn on minecraft hardcore mode, its a one life system. If you die once, your world gets deleted. It adds a much needed challenge, espcially when you add friends into the mix.
